## Audience

- **Primary user:** Small business owners — founders and operators of companies under 50 people
- **Their core problem:** They're either burning out doing their own marketing with no system, or they've wasted money on agencies that delivered no measurable results. Almost all of them are throwing tactics at the wall with nothing that compounds.
- **Device context:** Both mobile and desktop equally — design must be excellent on both
- **Tech literacy:** Intermediate

## Ground Rules for This Project

> These apply to every prompt in this project. Never violate these.

- DO use `#B8FF47` as the primary accent — sparingly on key CTAs and highlights
- DO make it mobile-first and fully responsive
- DO write real, specific copy — no Lorem Ipsum, ever
- DO position against agencies explicitly — the site's tone is confident and direct
- DO NOT use purple gradients or generic SaaS color schemes
- DO NOT use Inter, Roboto, or Arial — use Syne and Plus Jakarta Sans only
- DO NOT add sections, pages, or features not explicitly requested
- DO NOT use stock photos of people smiling at laptops — no lifestyle photography
- DO NOT make this look like a typical agency site — it should feel systematized and editorial
- Output format: GoHighLevel AI Studio website — build section by section
